About Me

I am a recent graduate from NAIT's DMIT program where I specialized in Computer Software Development. Problem-solving is my jam - from coding solutions to puzzles in video games, I love to do it all. When I discovered coding (mostly thanks to customizable webpages on Neopets and MySpace) and the magic that came with it, I instantly fell in love.

I was a graphic designer for a number of years before deciding to pivot and take my career in a different direction. I'm passionate about helping others and being a part of effective solutions that help solve complex problems.

Outside of programming and graphic design, I have a house full of animals whom I love (2 dogs and 3 cats), I love to play video games (current addictions are Phasmophobia and the Resident Evil series), and I love to read (chick-lit books are my guilty pleasure).
