Hi! I'm Jill

I'm a Graphic Designer and Junior Software Developer residing in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada

About Me

I am a recent graduate from NAIT's DMIT program where I specialized in Computer Software Development. Problem-solving is my jam - from coding solutions to puzzles in video games, I love to do it all. When I discovered coding (mostly thanks to customizable webpages on Neopets and MySpace) and the magic that came with it, I instantly fell in love.

I'm also a graphic designer; with 9+ years of experience, I love to create fun and eye-catching graphics. I'm passionate about helping others and being a part of effective solutions that help solve complex problems - whether that be through design or programming!

When I'm not working on a programming or graphic design project, you can find me singing (loudly and out-of-tune) to 90s and early 2000s music (hello Aqua), cooking new recipes with my husband (we are huge foodies), playing video games (I collect Nintendo consoles and love to play games on my PC), or reading a book (chick-lit books are my guilty pleasure). I'm also a proud “mom” to 3 cats and 2 dogs - our friends and family kindly refer to our house as the “zoo”.
